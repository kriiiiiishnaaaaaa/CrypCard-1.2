FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about CryptoCard

How does USDC to fiat conversion work?+
When you swipe your card, we intercept the auth request in <200ms, fetch the live USDC/fiat rate from Chainlink, calculate the exact USDC to deduct, and approve the transaction. The rate is locked at that moment.
Which countries and currencies are supported?+
CryptoCard works in 180+ countries anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted. All major fiat currencies are supported including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, BRL, JPY, AUD, CAD and more.
Is there a minimum USDC deposit?+
The minimum deposit is $100 USDC. There is no maximum limit. We support TRC-20 (Tron) network for fast, low-fee deposits.
How long does it take to get my card?+
Your virtual card is issued after KYC verification. KYC review takes up to 3 working days. Once verified, your card details appear immediately in your dashboard.
Is my USDC safe if the platform goes down?+
Yes. CryptoCard uses a non-custodial architecture — your USDC stays in your self-custody wallet until you spend. Even if the platform goes offline, your USDC is always under your control.
Do I need KYC verification?+
Yes, KYC is legally required for card issuance. We use ZK-KYC technology — your identity is verified with zero-knowledge proofs. Your data is never stored on our servers or shared.
