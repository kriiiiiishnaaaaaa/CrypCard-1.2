Common Questions

Everything you need to know about CryptoCard

How does the USDC to fiat conversion work at point of sale? +
When you swipe your card, the merchant's terminal sends an authorization request to Visa/Mastercard. We intercept this via a webhook in <200ms, fetch the live USDC/fiat rate from Chainlink price feeds, calculate the exact USDC to deduct, and approve or decline the transaction. The rate is locked at that exact moment, so you always know what you're paying.
Which countries and currencies are supported? +
CryptoCard works in 180+ countries anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted. All major fiat currencies are supported including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, BRL, JPY, AUD, CAD and more. Your USDC is automatically converted to the local currency at the best available rate.
Is there a minimum USDC deposit amount? +
The minimum deposit is 10 USDC. There is no maximum deposit limit. For ERC-20 deposits, you'll need a small amount of ETH for gas fees. Base and Tron deposits have much lower network fees.
How long does it take to get my virtual card? +
Your virtual card is issued instantly after completing KYC verification. KYC typically takes 2â€“5 minutes using our automated AI-powered verification system. Once your identity is verified, your virtual card details appear immediately in your dashboard.
What happens if a transaction is declined? +
A transaction may be declined if your USDC balance is insufficient, you've exceeded your daily/monthly spend limit, or the merchant category is blocked in your settings. You'll receive an instant push notification explaining the reason. No USDC is deducted for declined transactions.
Is my USDC safe if the platform goes down? +
Yes. CryptoCard uses a non-custodial architecture â€” your USDC remains in your self-custody wallet until the exact moment you spend. We never hold your funds in our treasury. Even if the platform goes offline, your USDC is always under your control.
Do I need to complete KYC? +
Yes, KYC is legally required for card issuance in most jurisdictions. We use ZK-KYC technology, which means your identity is verified using zero-knowledge proofs. Your personal data is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties beyond the legal minimum.
Can I use this card for subscriptions and recurring payments? +
Yes! Your CryptoCard works for all recurring payments, subscriptions, and scheduled billing. Just ensure you maintain sufficient USDC balance. You can also set up automatic USDC top-ups to prevent subscription failures.
How do refunds work? +
Refunds are processed back to your USDC balance. The refund amount is calculated at the current USDC rate at the time of refund, not the original purchase rate â€” this is standard for all card products due to currency conversion. Refunds typically appear within 3â€“5 business days.
What is the conversion rate and when is it locked in? +
The conversion rate is sourced from Chainlink decentralized price feeds and locked at the exact millisecond your card authorization is processed. This prevents any slippage and ensures you pay exactly what you expect. The rate used is always displayed in your transaction details.
